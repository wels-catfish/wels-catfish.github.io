If you have been at least influenced by this and are curious to know more, there are several things you can do - all conveniently linked on one page! First of all...

The Nuclear Regulatory Commission is regulates nuclear power in the United States and regulates inspections. They are the go-to for anything dealing with nuclear power, especially on a governmental or federal level. Obviously, there will likely be a fairly pro-nuclear bias, but the NRC still should be a top source of information.

(This information is from their website.) The NRC is headquartered in Maryland. You can mail to:
