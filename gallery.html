Nuclear energy is so safe that the US government has to store incredible amounts of nuclear waste and spent fuel in one gigantic mountain in the middle of the Nevada desert that will be radioactive for the next million years. But don't take my word for it.

Nuclear energy is so safe that an entire town in Brazil needed to be cleaned and decontaminated from one aquamarine blue dust in a junkyard that turned out to be caesium chloride, killing 4 and radiating hundreds.

Nuclear energy is so safe that an entire forest near Chernobyl turned red from excitement! Just kidding, it was, and is, so radioactive that the trees mutated to produce red leaves and needles from the fallout. Nothing like a nice red tree like à la Dalí.

Nuclear energy is so safe that an entire team of skilled individuals was needed to clean up what we now consider to be a "minor accident" - you know, just in case it was still deadly. Wouldn't want to spread that radiation everywhere! But hey, it was only a minor accident, you know?
